About Pinelander Editions

Pinelander Editions produces accessible digital editions of 18th & 19th-century American survival and captivity narratives. Many of these texts are available only as image scans or unstructured files, limiting their readability and accessibility.

  • Fully searchable and compatible with modern e-readers
  • Edited to correct OCR errors and restore clear formatting
  • Styled with clean, readable typography
  • Designed to reflect the original structure and layout of the print edition
  • Packaged with custom covers and embedded metadata

Each book is prepared with attention to historical integrity and digital usability.
